Since 2018

A family kitchen
with ocean-side vibes.

Kololi Grill started as a beachside shack built by the Touray family. Today we're a full kitchen serving guests from every corner of The Gambia and beyond — without losing the warmth of home cooking.

Every dish uses produce from local farms and the Banjul fish market. We cook slow, we grill hot, and we try to leave you full and a little sun-kissed.
