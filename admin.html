HOW TO SET UP WHATSAPP BUSINESS CATALOG
1
Download WhatsApp Business app

Go to Google Play Store or Apple App Store and download WhatsApp Business (free). It's different from the regular WhatsApp app. Register with your business phone number.

2
Set up your business profile

Go to Settings → Business Profile. Add your business name, category, address, email and description. Upload your logo as your profile photo.

3
Open the Catalog

Go to Settings → Business Tools → Catalog. Tap the + button to add your first product.

Settings → Business Tools → Catalog → Add Item (+)
4
Add each product

For every product, add Photo (use the photos from your catalog), Name, Price (in GMD), and Description. You can add up to 500 items.

📷 Photo📝 Name💰 Price📄 Description
5
Share your catalog

Once your catalog is set up, customers can see it by visiting your WhatsApp profile. You can also share individual products by tapping the product and then Share. Each product gets its own share link.

6
Use the HTML catalog for your website

Download the catalog from this tool and host it on your website via Cloudflare Pages. Customers can browse online too.
