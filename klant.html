Frequently Asked Questions
How can I customize my website?

Send a WhatsApp message to your staff member with the changes you want (text, photos, hours). Your website will be updated within 24 hours.

Can I downgrade my plan?

Yes, go to the Subscription tab and choose a lower plan. Tools that are no longer included will be paused, but your data is preserved.

How do I pay via Afrimoney?

Our staff member will send you a payment link via WhatsApp. You can easily pay using the Afrimoney app on your phone. Wave and QMoney are also available.

What happens if my subscription expires?

Your website stays active until the end of your paid period. After that, it goes offline, but your data is preserved for 90 days so you can always reactivate it.

Can I use a custom domain name?

Yes! Contact your staff member. We'll help you connect your own .gm or .com domain to your website.
